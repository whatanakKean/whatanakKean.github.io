## What is Data?

At its core, **data** is a collection of raw facts, numbers, or observations that can be processed to generate meaningful information.

Examples:

- Numbers in a spreadsheet
- Customer purchase history
- Social media likes and comments
- Temperature readings from sensors
- Website traffic logs

Raw data on its own may not make sense. But when processed, analyzed, and interpreted, it becomes powerful information.

## The Data Lifecycle

Data goes through several stages before delivering value.

### 1. Collection

Gathering raw data from various sources.

### 2. Storage

Storing data in:

- Databases
- Data warehouses
- Data lakes
- Cloud storage systems

### 3. Processing

Cleaning, transforming, and preparing data for analysis.

### 4. Analysis

Applying statistical methods or machine learning techniques.

### 5. Visualization & Reporting

Presenting insights through dashboards, charts, and reports.

### 6. Decision Making

Using insights to guide business strategies.
